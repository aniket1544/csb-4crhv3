The role of emotional resilience in mental health.

Resilience can be the ability of an individual to cope up with life difficulties and from mental health issues like depression, anxiety, stress, traumas, phobias. People who are having ability of resilience may thrive back to life with enthusiasm and joy because they might cope up with challenges, difficulties and even worst case scenarios in life. Resilience can be the ability to heal from mental illness. People having the quality of resilience could be open to challenges and can easily accept challenges in life. They can have capacity to accept, face, navigate difficult situations and can be very flexible.
