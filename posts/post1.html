Resilience can be the ability of an individual to cope up with life difficulties and from mental health issues like depression, anxiety, stress, traumas, phobias. People who are having ability of resilience may thrive back to life with enthusiasm and joy because they might cope up with challenges, difficulties and even worst case scenarios in life. Resilience can be the ability to heal from mental illness. People having the quality of resilience could be open to challenges and can easily accept challenges in life. They can have capacity to accept, face, navigate difficult situations and can be very flexible. People with strong emotional resilience can manage their emotional health even if they face stress, anxiety, depression. They can have the ability to cope up with the challenges by themselves and an individual with the quality of resilience might be very good at problem solving. He or she might find solutions very easily and difficult situations can be handled with the help of friends, family members, the people who support, relatives or even professional or guides. Individuals who have the ability of resilience can be very self aware and they can know their weaknesses and strengths as well, they could know their self esteem, self worth, they may have a lot of self knowledge and they can make use of this knowledge into cultivating positive relationships or it can also help in personal growth. Individuals accept that change can be the part of life and it cannot be avoided so staying positive and happy and accepting the upcoming situations, challenges and change in life might be the ability of resilience. Ability of resilience can be very important in building good mental health.
